About me:

Hi, I'm Bas

Kia Ora all! My name is Sebastian Chapman, but I normally go by Seb or Bas. I’ve been living down in Queenstown for the last few years and am originally from the mighty Manawatu.

As for something about my values, I have two that drive me. Firstly, I think mental health is a monumental issue in our day and age, and the thing that I value the most and has personally helped me a lot is taking care of some of the very human needs that we all have. Those needs that I value are the following: Taking time to be in nature, exercising, being social and being part of a community. My other core driving value does rely on the first one being in place so that it can function. The world we live in is facing the biggest challenges in its history with the war against climate change and without having healthy, happy, fully function humans, we won’t have a fighting chance. Which leads me onto my other driving value. The principal of creating economic systems that heal and nurture the planet and all the ecosystems that find themselves at home on this flying rock.

All of that leads to why I’m here at Dev-Academy! I believe tech provides the biggest lever to make a difference on both of those fronts. Learning how to code will hopefully give me a good set of tools to have a crack at making a difference, and hey, even if I don’t manage to, coding can be a pretty fun way to spend your days!

Thanks for reading my blog!
